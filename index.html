the course
BUILD THE BRAINS BEHIND MODERN AI

Transformers and large language models power today's most advanced AI — from ChatGPT to real-time translators and creative tools. In this course, you'll go beyond the hype to master the core technologies driving this revolution.

Through hands-on training and real-world applications, you'll learn how to build, fine-tune, and deploy custom generative models. From the architecture of transformers to the latest LLM acceleration tricks, this course gives you the tools to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving field.

Transformer Models & LLM Foundations
Core principles of modern generative AI.
You'll start by understanding the architecture of transformers and attention mechanisms, then dive into models like BERT and GPT. These foundational tools are essential for working with today's LLMs.

Training Techniques & Challenges
How to train your own models.
Gain hands-on experience with the training process — including optimization strategies and the specific challenges involved in fine-tuning large transformer models on real-world data.

State-of-the-Art LLMs & Speed-ups
Making cutting-edge models usable.
Explore the latest developments in LLMs and the engineering tricks that make them efficient, including speed-up techniques like quantization, distillation, and specialized attention mechanisms.

Handling Long Inputs & Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
Extend your model's memory.
Transformers struggle with very long texts. Learn how to work around this using chunking, memory mechanisms, and RAG techniques that combine model outputs with external knowledge.

Beyond Text: Applications in Time Series
Transformers aren't just for language.
Discover how these models can also handle time series data — useful for forecasting, anomaly detection, and other tasks beyond NLP.

Diffusion Models & Conditional Generation
Image generation and guided creativity.
Learn how generative diffusion models create content from noise, and how to steer generation using conditions like prompts, tags, or structured input.

Mixture of Experts & Multimodal Learning
Next-gen model architectures.
Explore models that dynamically select “experts” for tasks, and systems that combine different data types (text, image, audio) for richer understanding and generation.
